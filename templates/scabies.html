Scabies Products

Scabies is a highly contagious skin infestation caused by the Sarcoptes scabiei mite. It is characterized by intense itching and a pimple-like rash. Treatment for scabies typically involves prescription medications, such as topical creams or oral medications, that kill the mites and their eggs. It is also important to wash all clothes, bedding, and towels in hot water to prevent reinfection.
