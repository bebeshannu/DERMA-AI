Keratosis Products

Keratosis refers to a group of skin conditions characterized by the buildup of keratin, a protein found in the skin. These conditions can include actinic keratosis, seborrheic keratosis, and keratosis pilaris. Treatment for keratosis depends on the specific type and severity of the condition but may include topical medications, cryotherapy, laser therapy, or surgical removal. It's important to consult with a dermatologist for proper diagnosis and treatment.
