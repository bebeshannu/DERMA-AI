Dermatofibroma Products

Dermatofibroma is a benign skin growth that commonly appears on the lower legs but can occur in other areas. It presents as a firm, round, brownish to purplish nodule, often less than 1 centimeter in diameter. Although dermatofibromas are harmless and usually painless, they may itch or be tender. Treatment is typically not necessary unless the growth becomes bothersome. However, some people may opt for removal for cosmetic reasons.
